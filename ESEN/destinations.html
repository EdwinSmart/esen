Discover Our Destinations

Immerse yourself in the beauty of these amazing destinations. Choose your next adventure with Socotra Tours and Travel.

  • Maasai Mara, Kenya

    Maasai Mara is a wildlife reserve in southwestern Kenya, adjacent to Tanzania. It is famous for its exceptional population of lions, leopards, and cheetahs.

  • Watamu Malindi, Kenya

    Watamu and Malindi are coastal towns in Kenya known for their beautiful beaches and vibrant marine life. Explore the coral reefs and relax on pristine sandy shores.

  • Lake Nakuru National Park, Kenya

    Lake Nakuru National Park is a paradise for bird watchers. Enjoy the breathtaking views of the lake and spot diverse bird species and wildlife.
